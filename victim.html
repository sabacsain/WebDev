WHAT TO DO IF YOU HAVE BEEN A VICTIM OF ONLINE FRAUD

1. You can file a complaint immediately using the Internet Crime Complaint Center, Fraud.org, Better Business Bureau, Report a Concern page, and US Postal Inspection Service

2. Contact law-enforcement individuals in your city and state or the fraudster’s city and state.
