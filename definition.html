DEFINITION OF ONLINE FRAUD

Online Fraud is fraud committed using the internet, as we know the internet has many components such as chats, emails, and websites that people utilize daily for different purposes are also being used by scammers to commit fraud.

Online Fraud involves scams such as phishing, identity theft, hacking activities, viruses, denial of service, etc. which earns scammers a lot of money from their victims. This kind of fraud continues to grow as more and more people use the internet and cyber-criminal techniques become more sophisticated.
